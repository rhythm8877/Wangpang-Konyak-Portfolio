About Me

Dedicated Public Servant

Born and raised with deep roots in Mon district, I have dedicated my life to public service and community welfare. With a Master's degree in Political Science from North Eastern Hill University (NEHU), I bring both academic knowledge and grassroots understanding to my role as a public representative.

My journey in social work began long before entering politics, driven by a commitment to uplift marginalized communities and ensure inclusive development. As the MLA of Tapi (ST) constituency and Advisor to the Chief Minister on Social Welfare, I work tirelessly to bridge the gap between policy and implementation.

My vision centers on integrity, transparency, and community-driven development, with special focus on youth empowerment, healthcare accessibility, and social welfare programs that reach every corner of our constituency.
